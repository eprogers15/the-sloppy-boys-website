Vesper

Ingredients

  • Gin (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Vodka (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lillet Blanc (.25oz/7.5ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with lemon zest.
