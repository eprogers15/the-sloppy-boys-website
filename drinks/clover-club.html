Clover Club

Ingredients

  • Gin (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Raspberry Syrup (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Egg White (few drops)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, strain into chilled cocktail glass.
  2. Garnish with fresh raspberries.
