Mai Tai (Don the Beachcomber's Version)

Ingredients

  • Dark Jamaican Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Gold Cuban Rum (1oz/30ml)
  • Grapefruit Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Cointreau (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lime Juice (.75oz/22ml)
  • Falernum (1oz/30ml)
  • Pernod (6 drops)
  • Angostura (1 dash)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake vigorously and strain into an old fashioned glass filled with shaved or pebble ice.
  3. Garnish with mint sprig.
