Brooklyn

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey (2oz/60ml)
  • Dry Vermouth (1oz/30ml)
  • Maraschino Liqueur (.25oz/7ml)
  • Amer Picon* (.25oz/7ml)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into mixing glass with ice and stir.
  2. Strain into coupe glass.
  3. Garnish with skewered maraschino cherry.

Notes

  • Episode 36 - Brooklyn (June 25, 2021)
  • *May substitute with 2 dashes Angostura bitters.
