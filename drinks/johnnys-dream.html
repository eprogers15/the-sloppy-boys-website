Johnny's Dream

Ingredients

  • Silver Tequila ("2 parts" (2oz/60ml))
  • Aperol ("1 part" (1oz/30ml))
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Orange Bitters (4-5 drops)
  • Orange Juice (1 squeeze)

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into a cocktail glass.
  3. Serve up with an orange twist.
