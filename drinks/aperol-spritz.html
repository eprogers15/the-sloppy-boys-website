Aperol Spritz

Ingredients

  • Prosecco (3oz/90ml)
  • Aperol (2oz/60ml)
  • Club Soda (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a wine glass filled with ice and stir.
  2. Garnish with orange.
