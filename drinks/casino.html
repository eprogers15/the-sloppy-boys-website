Casino

Ingredients

  • Old Tom Gin (1.33oz/40ml)
  • Maraschino Liqueur (.33oz/10ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.33oz/10ml)
  • Orange Bitters (2 dashes)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker and shake well with ice.
  2. Strain into chilled rocks glass with ice.
  3. Garnish with lemon zest and maraschino cherry.
