Zombie

Ingredients

  • Jamaican Dark Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Gold Puerto Rican Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Demerara Rum (1oz/30ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (1 dash)
  • Falernum (.5oz/15ml)
  • Donn's Mix* (.5/15ml)
  • Grenadine (1 tsp)
  • Lime Juice (.66oz/20ml)
  • Pernod (6 drops)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into blender with 1/2 cup cracked ice.
  2. Blend for a few seconds.
  3. Serve in a tall tumbler glass.
  4. Garnish with mint leaves.

Notes

  • Episode 2 - Zombie (October 30, 2020)
  • IBA Zombie Recipe
  • *Donn's Mix = 2 parts yellow grapefruit juice, 1 part cinnamon syrup.
