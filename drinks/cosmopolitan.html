Cosmopolitan

Ingredients

  • Vodka Citron (1.35oz/40ml)
  • Cointreau (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Cranberry Juice (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into large cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with lemon twist.
