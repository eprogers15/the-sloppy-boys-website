Sex on the Beach

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.33oz/40ml)
  • Peach Schnapps (.66oz/20ml)
  • Orange Juice (1.33oz/40ml)
  • Cranberry Juice (1.33oz/40ml)

Steps

  1. Build all ingredients in a highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Garnish with half orange slice.
