Dark 'n' Stormy

Ingredients

  • Goslings Rum (2oz/60ml)
  • Ginger Beer (3.33oz/100ml)

Steps

  1. In a highball glass filled with ice pour the ginger beer and top floating with the Rum.
  2. Garnish with a lime wedge or slice.
