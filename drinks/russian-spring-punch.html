Russian Spring Punch

Ingredients

  • Vodka (.8oz/25ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.8oz/25ml)
  • Creme de Cassis (.5oz/15ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.33oz/10ml)
  • Sparkling Wine (top up)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients (except sparkling wine) into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake well with ice, strain into a chilled tall glass filled with ice, and top up with wine.
  3. Garnish with blackberries and optionally, a lemon slice.
