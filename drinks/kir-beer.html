Kir Beer

Ingredients

  • Miller High Life (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Creme de Cassis (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Open the bottle and drink down part of the neck.
  2. Add Creme de Cassis juice into the bottle.
  3. Mix lightly.
  4. Enjoy in the bottle or pour out into your favorite cocktail glass.
