Hot Buttered Rum

Ingredients

  • Jamaican Rum (2oz/60ml)
  • Powdered Sugar (1 tsp)
  • Unsalted Butter ("half a chestnut")

Steps

  1. Use a medium bar glass, hot.
  2. Dissolve the sugar in a little boiling water.
  3. Add rum and butter, fill the glass two-thirds full of boiling water and stir.
  4. Garnish with grated nutmeg.
