Brandy Alexander

Ingredients

  • Cognac (1oz/30ml)
  • Creme De Cacao (1oz/30ml)
  • Fresh Cream (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into shaker filled with ice cubes.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with fresh ground nutmeg.
