Sea Breeze

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.33oz/40ml)
  • Cranberry Juice (4oz/120ml)
  • Grapefruit Juice (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Build all ingredients in a highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Garnish with orange zest and cherry.
