Blue Hawaii

Ingredients

  • Vodka (.75oz/22ml)
  • Light Rum (.75oz/22ml)
  • Blue Curacao (.5oz/15ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (3oz/90ml)
  • Sweet and Sour (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into hurricane glass over ice.
  3. Garnish with pineapple slice and cocktail umbrella.
