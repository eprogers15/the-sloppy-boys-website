Acid-Adjusted Orange Juice Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Acid-Adjusted Orange Juice (.8oz/25ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.66oz/20ml)
  • Egg White (.66oz/20ml)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients into a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well.
  3. Strain into a cobbler glass, or an old fashioned glass filled with ice.
  4. *To adjust orange juice, add 52 grams of citric acid powder per liter.
