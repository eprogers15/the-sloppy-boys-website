Tipperary

Ingredients

  • Irish Whiskey (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Sweet Red Vermouth (.85oz/25ml)
  • Green Chartreuse (.5oz/15ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (2 dashes)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir well.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with an orange slice.
