Ranch Water

Ingredients

  • Topo Chico (12oz bottle)
  • Tequila Blanco (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Open the bottle and drink down part of the neck.
  2. Add tequila and lime juice into the bottle.
  3. Mix lightly.
  4. Enjoy in the bottle or pour out into your favorite cocktail glass.
