Champagne Cocktail

Ingredients

  • Champagne (3oz/90ml)
  • Cognac (.33oz/10ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (2 dashes)
  • Grand Marnier (few drops, optional)
  • Sugar Cube (1)

Steps

  1. Place the sugar cube and bitters in a large Champagne glass and add the cognac.
  2. Pour gently chilled Champagne.
  3. Garnish with orange zest and maraschino cherry.
