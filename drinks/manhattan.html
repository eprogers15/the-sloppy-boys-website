Manhattan

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Sweet Red Vermouth (.67oz/20ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (1 dash)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir well.
  3. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with cocktail cherry.
