Corpse Reviver #2

Ingredients

  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Cointreau (1oz/30ml)
  • Lillet Blanc (1oz/30ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Absinthe (1 dash)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with orange zest.
