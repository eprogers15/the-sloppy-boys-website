Fernandito

Ingredients

  • Fernet Branca (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Coca-Cola (top up)

Steps

  1. Pour the Fernet Branca into a double old fashioned glass with ice, fill the glass up with cola.
  2. Gently stir.
