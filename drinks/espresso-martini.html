Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.66oz/50ml)
  • Kahlua (1oz/30ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.33oz/10ml)
  • Espresso (1 (strong))

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker and shake well with ice.
  2. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
