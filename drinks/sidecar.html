Sidecar

Ingredients

  • Cognac (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Triple Sec (.7oz/20ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.7oz/20ml)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake well with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
