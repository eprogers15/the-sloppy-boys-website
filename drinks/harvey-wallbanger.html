Harvey Wallbanger

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Galliano (.5oz/15ml)
  • Orange Juice (3oz/90ml)

Steps

  1. Stir vodka and orange juice in a highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Float Galliano on top.
  3. Garnish with cherry and orange slice.
