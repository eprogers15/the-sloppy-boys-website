Paradise

Ingredients

  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Apricot Brandy (.66oz/20ml)
  • Orange Juice (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake well with ice.
  3. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
