Spiked Watermelon

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1 pint/375ml)
  • Watermelon (1 whole)

Steps

  1. Inject or insert one pint bottle (375ml) of vodka into a whole watermelon.
  2. Allow melon to rest refrigerated for 24 hours before serving.
