Dry Martini

Ingredients

  • Gin (2oz/60ml)
  • Dry Vermouth (.33oz/10ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir Well.
  3. Strain into chilled martini cocktail glass.
  4. Squeeze oil from lemon peel onto the drink, or garnish with green olives if requested.
