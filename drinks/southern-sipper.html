Southern Sipper

Ingredients

  • Bourbon (2oz/60ml)
  • Peach Schnapps (1oz/30ml)
  • Peychaud's Bitters (1 fat dash)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients in an Old Fashioned glass full of ice.
  2. Stir until melty.
  3. Enjoy alone in the dark.
