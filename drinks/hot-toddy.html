Hot Toddy

Ingredients

  • Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Hot Water (1 cup)
  • Lemon Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Honey (1 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a warmed mug.
  2. Garnish with lemon wedge, cinnamon stick, clove or star anise.
