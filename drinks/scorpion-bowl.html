Scorpion Bowl

Ingredients

  • Light Rum (6oz/180ml)
  • Brandy (1oz/30ml)
  • Orange Juice (6oz/180ml)
  • Lemon Juice (4oz/120ml)
  • Orgeat (1.5oz/45ml)

Steps

  1. Blend ingredients in a mixer with two scoops shaved ice.
  2. Pour into scorpion bowl.
  3. Fill with ice and granish with fruits and gardenias.
  4. Add long straws.
  5. For flame, soak a sugar cube in overproof rum and light once bowl is placed on table.
