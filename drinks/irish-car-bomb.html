Tom Collins

Ingredients

  • Gin (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.5oz/15ml)
  • Soda Water (2oz/60ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients directly into highball filled with ice.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Garnish with lemon slice and maraschino cherry.
