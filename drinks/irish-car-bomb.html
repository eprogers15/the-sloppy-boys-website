Irish Car Bomb

Ingredients

  • Guinness (half pint)
  • Irish Whiskey (.5oz/15ml)
  • Baileys Irish Creme (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Fill shot glass with equal parts whiskey and Baileys.
  2. Drop into pint glass half-filled with Guinness and drink before curdling.
