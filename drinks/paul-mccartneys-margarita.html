Paul McCartney's Margarita

Ingredients

  • White Tequila (3 shots)
  • Cointreau (1 shot)
  • Triple Sec (1 shot)
  • Lime Juice (from one lime)
  • Orange Juice (from one orange)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into shaker with 2 or 3 small ice cubes.
  2. Shake until cubes melt and drink is frothy.
  3. Pour into glass with salted rim.
