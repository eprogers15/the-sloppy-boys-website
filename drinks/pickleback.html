Pickleback

Ingredients

  • Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Pickle Brine (1.5oz/45ml)

Steps

  1. Add whiskey and pickle brine into separate shot glasses.
  2. Take the whiskey as a shot, then the pickle brine as a chaser.
