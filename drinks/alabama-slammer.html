Alabama Slammer

Ingredients

  • Southern Comfort (1oz/30ml)
  • Sloe Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Amaretto Liqueur (1oz/30ml)
  • Orange Juice (2oz/60ml)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake until well-chilled.
  3. Strain into a highball glass over fresh ice. Or, if serving as a shot, divide among shot glasses.
  4. Garnish with orange wedge.
