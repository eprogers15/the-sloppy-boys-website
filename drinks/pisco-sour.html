Pisco Sour

Ingredients

  • Pisco (2oz/60ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.66oz/20ml)
  • Egg White (1)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled goblet glass.
  3. Garnish with a few dashes of Amargo bitters.
