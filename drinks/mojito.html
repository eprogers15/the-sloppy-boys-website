Mojito

Ingredients

  • White Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lime Juice (.66oz/20ml)
  • Mint (6 sprigs)
  • Sugar (2 tsp)
  • Soda Water

Steps

  1. Muddle mint, sugar and lime juice.
  2. Add splash of soda and fill glass with ice.
  3. Pour rum and top with soda.
  4. Stir lightly to involve all ingredients.
