Pimm's Cup

Ingredients

  • Pimm's No. 1 (2oz/60ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Ginger Ale (to top)

Steps

  1. Add Pimm's No. 1 and lemon juice into a highball glass over ice.
  2. Top with ginger ale and stir briefly to combine.
  3. Garnish with a cucumber slice, mint sprig, strawberry and optional lemon and orange wheels.
