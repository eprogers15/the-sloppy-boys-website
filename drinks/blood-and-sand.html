Blood and Sand

Ingredients

  • Scotch (.75oz/22ml)
  • Sweet Vermouth (.75oz/22ml)
  • Cherry Heering (.75oz/22ml)
  • Orange Juice (.75oz/22ml)

Steps

  1. Add scotch, sweet vermouth, cherry liqueur and orange juice into a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled coupe or cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with orange peel.
