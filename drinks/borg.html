BORG

Ingredients

  • Water (1 gallon jug)
  • Vodka Juice (750ml bottle)
  • MiO (a few squirts)

Steps

  1. Pour out (or drink) half the gallon of water.
  2. Fill the remainder with vodka.
  3. Add several servings of MiO, Liquid IV Hydration Enhancer, or your preferred flavoring.
  4. Give your borg a name and label it in permanent marker.
