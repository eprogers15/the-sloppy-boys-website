Cuba Libre (Rum and Coke)

Ingredients

  • White Rum (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Coca-Cola (4oz/120ml)
  • Lime Juice (.33oz/10ml)

Steps

  1. Build all ingredients into a highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Garnish with lime wedge.
