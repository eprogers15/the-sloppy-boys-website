Suffering Bastard

Ingredients

  • Cognac or Brandy (1oz/30ml)
  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (2 dashes)
  • Ginger Beer (top up)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients (except ginger beer) into a shaker, shake well with ice and pour unstrained into Bastard mug.
  2. Top up with ginger beer.
  3. Garnish with mint and orange slice.
