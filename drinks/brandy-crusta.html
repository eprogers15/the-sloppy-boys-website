Brandy Crusta

Ingredients

  • Brandy (1.75oz/52.5ml)
  • Maraschino Liqueur (.25oz/7.5ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Curacao (1 barspoon)
  • Simple Syrup (1 barspoon)
  • Aromatic Bitters (2 dashes)

Steps

  1. Rub a slice of orange (or lemon) around the rim of a slim cocktail glass and dip it in pulverized white sugar, so that the sugar adheres to the edge of the glass.
  2. Carefully curl the orange/lemon peel around the inside of the glass.
  3. Then, combine ingredients with ice in a mixing glass. Stir and strain into the prepared glass.
