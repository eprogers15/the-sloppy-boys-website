Americano

Ingredients

  • Bitter Campari (1oz/30ml)
  • Sweet Red Vermouth (1oz/30ml)
  • Soda Water (splash)

Steps

  1. Mix Campari and vermouth in an old fashioned glass filled with ice cubes.
  2. Add a splash of soda water.
  3. Stir gently.
  4. Garnish with half orange slice and a lemon zest.
