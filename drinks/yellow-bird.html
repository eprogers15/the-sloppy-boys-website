Yellow Bird

Ingredients

  • White Rum (1oz/30ml)
  • Galliano (.5oz/15ml)
  • Triple Sec (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
