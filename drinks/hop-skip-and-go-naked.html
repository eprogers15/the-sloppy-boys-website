Hop, Skip & Go Naked

Ingredients

  • Beer (6 cans or bottles)
  • Whiskey (1 pint)
  • Frozen Lemonade Concentrate (1 tube)

Steps

  1. In a large container combine beer, lemonade and whiskey.
  2. Stir and serve over ice.
