Adios Motherfucker

Ingredients

  • Tequila (.5oz/15ml)
  • Vodka (.5oz/15ml)
  • White Rum (.5oz/15ml)
  • Gin (.5oz/15ml)
  • Blue Curacao (.5oz/15ml)
  • Sweet & Sour (2oz/60ml)
  • Sprite or 7UP (top up)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Garnish with lemon wedge and cherry.
