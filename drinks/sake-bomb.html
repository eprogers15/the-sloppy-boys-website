Sake Bomb

Ingredients

  • Japanese Beer (pint)
  • Sake (shot)

Steps

  1. Fill half a pint glass with Japanese beer and place chopsticks across the top of the glass.
  2. Fill a shot glass with sake and carefully balance on top of the chopsticks.
  3. When ready to drink, pound the table next to the glass until the shot falls into the beer.
