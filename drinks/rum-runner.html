Rum Runner

Ingredients

  • Light Rum (1oz/30ml)
  • Navy-Strength Rum (1oz/30ml)
  • Banana Liqueur (1oz/30ml)
  • Blackberry Liqueur (.5oz/15ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (2oz/60ml)
  • Lime Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Grenadine (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into hurricane glass filled with ice.
  3. Garnish with skewered brandied cherry and pineapple wedge.
