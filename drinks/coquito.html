Coquito

Ingredients

  • Condensed Milk (1 can)
  • Evaporated Milk (1 can)
  • Cream of Coconut (1 can)
  • White Rum (half cup)
  • Vanilla Extract (.5 tsp)
  • Cinnamon (1 tsp, ground)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend on high until mixture is well combined for 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Transfer mixture into glass bottles and chill in the refrigerator until cold.
  4. To serve, pour coquito into small serving glasses (almost a shot portion) and garnish with ground cinnamon or a cinnamon stick.
