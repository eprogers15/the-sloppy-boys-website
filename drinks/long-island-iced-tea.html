Long Island Iced Tea

Ingredients

  • Tequila (.5oz/15ml)
  • Vodka (.5oz/15ml)
  • White Rum (.5oz/15ml)
  • Triple Sec (.5oz/15ml)
  • Gin (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.75oz/25ml)
  • Gomme Syrup (1oz/30ml)
  • Cola (dash)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into highball glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Garnish with lemon spiral.
  4. Serve with straw.
