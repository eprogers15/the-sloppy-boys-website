Mulled Wine

Ingredients

  • Red Wine (4.25cup/1L)
  • Brandy (1.25cup/10oz)
  • Large Oranges (2)
  • Cardamom Pods (3, slightly crushed)
  • Brown Sugar (half cup)
  • Whole Cloves (6)
  • Cinnamon Sticks (2)

Steps

  1. Peel strips from one orange and juice the orange. Slice the other orange into rounds for garnish.
  2. Slice the other orange into rounds for garnish.
  3. In a nonreactive saucepan, combine orange peel, juice and remaining ingredients.
  4. Stir over medium heat until the sugar dissolves (about 2 mins.).
  5. Increase heat and bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low.
  6. Simmer for 30 mins.
  7. Strain and discard the solids.
  8. Ladle into cups or mugs.
  9. Garnish with orange round.
