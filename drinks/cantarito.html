Cantarito

Ingredients

  • Tequila (2oz/60ml)
  • Orange Juice (2oz/60ml)
  • Grapefruit Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Lime Juice (.75oz/22ml)
  • Grapefruit Soda (a dash)
  • Salt (a pinch)

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients (except grapefruit soda) in a cocktail shaker and shake with ice.
  2. Pour into clay mug or highball glass.
  3. Top up with grapefruit soda.
  4. Garnish with lime wedge and grapefruit wedge.
