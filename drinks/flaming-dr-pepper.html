Flaming Dr. Pepper

Ingredients

  • Beer (8oz/230ml)
  • Amaretto (.75oz/22ml)
  • Overproof Rum (.25oz/7ml)

Steps

  1. Fill pint glass halfway with beer.
  2. Add amaretto to a shot glass and top with rum.
  3. Light the rum on fire and carefully drop shot glass into the beer.
