Sazerac

Ingredients

  • Cognac* (1.66oz/50ml)
  • Absinthe (.33oz/10ml)
  • Peychaud's Bitters (2 dashes)
  • Sugar Cube (1)

Steps

  1. Rinse a chilled old-fashioned glass with absinthe, add crushed ice and set aside.
  2. Stir remaining ingredients over ice in a mixing glass.
  3. Discard the ice and excess absinthe from the prepared glass, strain the mixed drink into the glass.

Notes

  • Episode 71 - Sazerac (February 25, 2022)
  • IBA Sazerac Recipe
  • *Today, rye whiskey is most common.
