Bee's Knees

Ingredients

  • Dry Gin (1.77oz/52.5ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.75oz/22.5ml)
  • Orange Juice (.75oz/22.5ml)
  • Honey Syrup (2 tsp)

Steps

  1. Stir honey syrup with lemon and orange juices until it dissolves, add gin and shake with ice.
  2. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
