Spagett

Ingredients

  • Miller High Life (12oz bottle)
  • Aperol (1oz/30ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Open the bottle and drink down part of the neck.
  2. Add Aperol and lemon juice into the bottle.
  3. Mix lightly.
  4. Enjoy in the bottle or pour out into your favorite cocktail glass
