Irish Coffee

Ingredients

  • Irish Whiskey (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Hot Coffee (4oz/120ml)
  • Fresh Cream (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Sugar (1 tsp)

Steps

  1. Pour coffee into pre-heated Irish coffee glass.
  2. Add whiskey and sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. Float fresh thick chilled cream on the surface without mixing.
