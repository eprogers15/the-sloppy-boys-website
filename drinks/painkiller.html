Painkiller

Ingredients

  • Pusser's Rum (2oz/60ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (4oz/120ml)
  • Orange Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Cream of Coconut (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Add ingredients into a shaker with cubed ice and shake vigorously but briefly to combine.
  2. Strain into a hurricane glass or snifter over crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with nutmeg and pineapple wedge.
