Mint Julep

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey (2oz/60ml)
  • Fresh Mint (4 sprigs)
  • Powdered Sugar (1 tsp)
  • Water (2 tsp)

Steps

  1. In Julep stainless steel cup, gently muddle the mint with sugar and water.
  2. Fill the glass with cracked ice.
  3. Add bourbon and stir well.
  4. Garnish with mint.
