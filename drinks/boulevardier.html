Boulevardier

Ingredients

  • Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Campari (1oz/30ml)
  • Sweet Red Vermouth (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir well.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with orange zest or lemon zest.
