Margarita

Ingredients

  • Tequila (1.7oz/50ml)
  • Triple Sec (.66oz/20ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients into a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into ice filled margarita glass rimmed with salt (optional).
  3. Garnish with lime wedge.
