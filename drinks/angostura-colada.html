Angostura Colada

Ingredients

  • Angostura Bitters (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Overproof Rum (.5oz/15ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (2oz/60ml)
  • Lime Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Cream of Coconut (1.5oz/45ml)

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a large snifter over crushed ice.
  3. Garnish with nutmeg and pineapple fronds.
