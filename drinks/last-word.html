Last Word

Ingredients

  • Gin (.75oz/22.5ml)
  • Green Chartreuse (.75oz/22.5ml)
  • Maraschino Liqueur (.75oz/22.5ml)
  • Lime Juice (.75oz/22.5ml)

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
