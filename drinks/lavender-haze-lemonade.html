Lavender Haze Lemonade

Ingredients

  • Empress 1908 Gin (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Falernum Liqueur (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Rhubarb Bitters (3 dashes)
  • Elderflower Tonic (to top)

Steps

  1. Add gin, falernum and lemon juice into a shaker.
  2. Add bitters and a handful of ice.
  3. Shake and strain into a tall glass filled with ice and top off with tonic.
  4. Garnish with lemon peel.
