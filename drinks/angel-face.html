Angel Face

Ingredients

  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Apricot Brandy (1oz/30ml)
  • Calvados (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker filled with ice cubes.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
