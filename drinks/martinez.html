Martinez

Ingredients

  • London Dry Gin (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Sweet Red Vermouth (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Maraschino Liqueur (1 bar spoon)
  • Orange Bitters (2 dashes)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir well.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with lemon zest.
