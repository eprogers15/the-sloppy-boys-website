Sidewalk Slammer

Ingredients

  • Malt Liquor (40oz)
  • Four Loko (1 can)

Steps

  1. Drink or dump half the contents of a 40oz malt liquor bottle.
  2. Add an entire Four Loko into the bottle.
  3. Gently move the bottle side to side to mix.
