New York Sour

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey or Bourbon (2oz/60ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.76oz/22.5ml)
  • Fresh Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Egg White (few drops)
  • Red Wine (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into the shaker.
  2. Shake vigorously with ice.
  3. Strain into a chilled rocks glass filled with ice.
  4. Float the wine on top.
  5. Garnish with lemon or orange zest with cherry.
