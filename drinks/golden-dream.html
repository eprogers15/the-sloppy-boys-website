Golden Dream

Ingredients

  • Orange Juice (.67oz/20ml)
  • Triple Sec (.67oz/20ml)
  • Galliano (.67oz/20ml)
  • Fresh Cream (.33oz/10ml)

Steps

  1. Pour orange juice into a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake briskly for a few seconds.
  3. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
