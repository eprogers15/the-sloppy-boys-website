Dirty Shirley

Ingredients

  • Vodka (2oz/60ml)
  • Grenadine (1oz/30ml)
  • Lemon-Lime Soda (8oz/240ml)

Steps

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add the vodka and grendadine to the glass.
  3. Top with soda and garnish with a maraschino cherry.
