Illegal

Ingredients

  • Espadin Mezcal (1oz/30ml)
  • Overproof White Rum (.5oz/15ml)
  • Falernum (.5oz/15ml)
  • Maraschino Luxardo (1 barspoon)
  • Lime Juice (.75oz/22.5ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.5oz/15ml)
  • Egg White (few drops, optional)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients into a shaker.
  2. Shake with ice and strain into a chilled cocktail glass, or serve on the rocks in a traditional clay or terracotta mug.
