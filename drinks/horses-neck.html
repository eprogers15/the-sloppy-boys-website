Horse's Neck

Ingredients

  • Cognac (1.33oz/40ml)
  • Ginger Ale (4oz/120ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (dash optional)

Steps

  1. Pour Cognac and ginger ale directly into a highball glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Add bitters if preferred.
  4. Garnish with a rind of lemon spiral.
