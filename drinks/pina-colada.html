Pina Colada

Ingredients

  • White Rum (1.75oz/50ml)
  • Coconut Cream (1oz/30ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (1.75oz/50ml)

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients with ice in an electric blender, pour into a large glass and serve with straws.
  2. Garnish with pineapple slice and a cocktail cherry.
