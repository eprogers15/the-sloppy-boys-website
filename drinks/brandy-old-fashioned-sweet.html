Brandy Old Fashioned Sweet

Ingredients

  • Brandy (2oz/60ml)
  • Orange Slices (2)
  • Sugar Cube (1)
  • Maraschino Cherries (2)
  • Angostura Bitters (3 dashes)
  • 7up/Sprite/Soda (to top)

Steps

  1. Muddle bitters, orange slices, cherries and sugar cube in an Old Fashioned glass.
  2. Add ice to fill glass, then brandy.
  3. Top up with 7up, Sprite or club soda.
  4. Stir to chill.
  5. Garnish with skewered cherry and an orange slice.
