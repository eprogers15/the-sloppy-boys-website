Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Sugar Cube (1)
  • Angostura Bitters (few dashes)
  • Water (few dashes)

Steps

  1. Place sugar cube in old fashioned glass and saturate with bitters and water.
  2. Muddle until dissolved.
  3. Add ice cubes and whiskey.
  4. Stir gently.
  5. Garnish with orange slice or zest, and cocktail cherry.
