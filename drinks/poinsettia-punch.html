Poinsettia Punch

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1 part)
  • Champagne (1 part)
  • Cranberry Juice (2 parts)

Steps

  1. Combine vodka and cranberry juice and chill.
  2. When ready to serve, add chilled champagne.
  3. Garnish with cranberries, rosemary and orange zest.
