Godfather

Ingredients

  • Scotch (1.2oz/35ml)
  • Amaretto (1.2oz/35ml)

Steps

  1. A peaty, smoky scotch is preferred.
  2. Pour all ingredients directly into an old fashioned glass filled with ice cubes.
  3. Stir gently.
