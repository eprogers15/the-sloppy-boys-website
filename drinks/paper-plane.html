Paper Plane

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey (1oz/30ml)
  • Amaro Nonino (1oz/30ml)
  • Aperol (1oz/30ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker, shake well with ice and strain into chilled cocktail glass.
