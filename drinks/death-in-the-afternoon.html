Death in the Afternoon

Ingredients

  • Absinthe (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Champagne (4oz/120ml)

Steps

  1. Pour one jigger of absinthe into a champagne glass.
  2. Add iced champagne until it attains the proper opalescent milkiness.
  3. Drink three to five of these slowly.
