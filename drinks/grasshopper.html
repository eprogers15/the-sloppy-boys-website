Grasshopper

Ingredients

  • Creme de Menthe (.66oz/20ml)
  • Creme de Cacao (.66oz/20ml)
  • Fresh Cream (.66oz/20ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake briskly for a few seconds.
  3. Strain into chilled cocktail glass.
