Seelbach

Ingredients

  • Old Forester Bourbon (1oz/30ml)
  • Triple Sec (.5oz/15ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (7 dashes)
  • Peychaud Bitters (7 dashes)
  • Korbel Brut (5oz/150ml)

Steps

  1. Combine bourbon, triple sec and bitters in a champagne flute and stir briefly.
  2. Pour in champagne.
  3. Twist an orange peel and rub rim of the flute.
  4. Drop the twist into the cocktail.
