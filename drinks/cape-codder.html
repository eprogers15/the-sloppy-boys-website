Cape Codder

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Cranberry Juice (to top)
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Pour vodka into highball glass over ice.
  2. Top with cranberry juice and stir briefly to combine.
  3. Squeeze lime wedge over drink and drop into drink.
