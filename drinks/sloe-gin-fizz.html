Sloe Gin Fizz

Ingredients

  • Sloe Gin (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.75oz/22ml)
  • Club Soda (to top)

Steps

  1. Add sloe gin, lemon juice and simple syrup into a cocktail shaker filled with ice and shake.
  2. Strain into a highball or Collins glass filled with ice.
  3. Top up with soda.
  4. Garnish with lemon wedge and cherry.
