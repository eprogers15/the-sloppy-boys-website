Porto Flip

Ingredients

  • Brandy (.5oz/15ml)
  • Red Tawny Port Wine (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Egg Yolk (.33oz/10ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  2. Sprinkle with fresh ground nutmeg.
