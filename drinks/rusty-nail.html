Rusty Nail

Ingredients

  • Scotch Whisky (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Drambuie (.85oz/25ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients directly into an old fashioned glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Garnish with lemon zest.
