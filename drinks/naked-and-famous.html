Naked and Famous

Ingredients

  • Mezcal (22.5ml/.75oz)
  • Aperol Juice (22.5ml/.75oz)
  • Yellow Chartreuse (22.5ml/.75oz)
  • Lime Juice (22.5ml/.75oz)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into chilled cocktail glass.
