Hugo Spritz

Ingredients

  • Prosecco (4oz/120ml)
  • St. Germain (.5oz/15ml)
  • Club Soda (1oz/30ml)
  • Mint (1 sprig)

Steps

  1. Add St. Germain and mint sprig into wine glass.
  2. Muddle gently and let sit for 3 minutes.
  3. Add ice, prosecco and soda and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a mint sprig and lemon wheel.
