Tuxedo

Ingredients

  • Old Tom Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Dry Vermouth (1oz/30ml)
  • Maraschino Luxardo (1/2 barspoon)
  • Absinthe (1/4 barspoon)
  • Orange Bitters (3 dashes)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir and strain into chilled martini cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with cherry and lemon zest.
