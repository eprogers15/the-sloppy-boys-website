Paloma

Ingredients

  • Agave Tequila (1.66oz/50ml)
  • Pink Grapefruit Soda (3.33oz/100ml)
  • Lime Juice (.2oz/5ml)
  • Salt (pinch)

Steps

  1. Pour tequila into a highball glass, squeeze the lime juice.
  2. Add ice and salt, fill with pink grapefruit soda.
  3. Stir gently.
  4. Garnish with lime slice.
