Yule Mule

Ingredients

  • Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Molasses Syrup* (.5oz/15ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Orange Bitters (1 dash)
  • Ginger Ale (to top)

Steps

  1. Shake ingredients (minus ginger ale) on ice and strain into an ice filled glass.
  2. Top up with ginger ale.
  3. Garnish with rosemary sprig and dash of nutmeg.

Notes

  • Episode 112 - Yule Mule (December 9, 2022)
  • *To make molasses syrup, combine equal parts water and blackstrap molasses in a saucepan on low heat (do not boil) for ten minutes. Use once cooled.
