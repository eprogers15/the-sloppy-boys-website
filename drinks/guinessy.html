Guinessy

Ingredients

  • Guinness (1 can)
  • Hennessy (1.75oz/50ml)

Steps

  1. Turn a Hennessy nip upside down into can of Guinness and shotgun it.
  2. Or, combine Guinness and Hennessy in any way that reflects your own personal style!
