Moscow Mule

Ingredients

  • Smirnoff Vodka (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Ginger Beer (4oz/120ml)
  • Lime Juice (.33oz/10ml)

Steps

  1. In a mule cup or rocks glass, combine vodka and ginger beer.
  2. Add lime juice and gently stir to involve all ingredients.
  3. Garnish with a lime slice.
