Whiskey Sour

Ingredients

  • Bourbon Whiskey (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Sugar Syrup (.5oz/15ml)
  • Egg White (dash optional)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into glass.
  3. Serve up or on the rocks.
  4. Garnish with orange slice and maraschino cherry.
