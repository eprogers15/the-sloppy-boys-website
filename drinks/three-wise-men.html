Three Wise Men

Ingredients

  • Johnnie Walker (.5oz/15ml)
  • Jack Daniels (.5oz/15ml)
  • Jim Beam (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Shake well with ice and strain into a shot glass.
