Fuzzy Navel

Ingredients

  • Peach Schnapps (3oz/90ml)
  • Orange Juice (3oz/90ml)

Steps

  1. Add peach schnapps and orange juice into a highball glass over ice.
  2. Stir briefly to combine.
  3. Garnish with an orange slice.
