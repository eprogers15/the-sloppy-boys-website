Lemon Drop Martini

Ingredients

  • Vodka Citron (1oz/30ml)
  • Triple Sec (.66oz/20ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker, shake well with ice and strain into cocktail glass.
  2. Garnish with sugar rim around the glass.
