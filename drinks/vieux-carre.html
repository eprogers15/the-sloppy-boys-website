Vieux Carre

Ingredients

  • Rye Whiskey (1oz/30ml)
  • Cognac (1oz/30ml)
  • Sweet Vermouth (1oz/30ml)
  • Benedictine (1 bar spoon)
  • Peychaud's Bitters (2 dashes)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into mixing glass with ice cubes.
  2. Stir well.
  3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  4. Garnish with orange zest and marachino cherry.
