Southside

Ingredients

  • London Dry Gin (2oz/60ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.5oz/15ml)
  • Mint Leaves (5-6)
  • Egg White (a few drops (optional))

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a cocktail shaker, shake well with ice, double-strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  2. If egg white is used, shake vigorously.
  3. Garnish with mint sprigs.
