Kalpy Cordial

Ingredients

  • Spiced Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Cherry Coke (to top)

Steps

  1. Pour rum in an old fashioned glass filled with ice.
  2. Top with cherry coke.
  3. Garnish or float in a manner that reflects your own personal styleez.
