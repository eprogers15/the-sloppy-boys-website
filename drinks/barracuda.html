Barracuda

Ingredients

  • Gold Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Galliano (.5oz/15ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (2oz/60ml)
  • Lime Juice (1 dash)
  • Prosecco (top up)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients (except Prosecco) into a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a chilled highball filled with ice.
  3. Top up with Prosecco.
  4. Garnish with pineapple and cherry, optional mint sprig for aroma.
