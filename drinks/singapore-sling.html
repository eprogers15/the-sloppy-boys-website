Singapore Sling

Ingredients

  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Pineapple Juice (4oz/120ml)
  • Cherry Liqueur (.5oz/15ml)
  • Angostura Bitters (1 dash)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Grenadine (.33oz/10ml)
  • Cointreau (.25oz/7ml)
  • Benedictine (.25oz/7ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into highball glass.
  3. Garnish with pineapple and cocktail cherry.
