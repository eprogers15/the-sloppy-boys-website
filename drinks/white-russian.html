White Russian

Ingredients

  • Vodka (1.75oz/50ml)
  • Coffee Liqueur (.66oz/20ml)
  • Fresh Cream

Steps

  1. Pour the ingredients into an old fashioned glass filled with ice cubes.
  2. Float fresh cream on top and stir in slowly.
