Planter's Punch

Ingredients

  • Jamaican Rum (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Lime Juice (.5oz/15ml)
  • Sugar Cane Juice (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into a small tumbler or a typical terracotta glass.
  2. Garnish with orange zest.
  3. Add dilution to taste. It can be given by water, ice or fresh juices.
