Tom & Jerry

Ingredients

  • Dark Rum (1oz/30ml)
  • Cognac (1oz/30ml)
  • Tom & Jerry Batter (1 tbsp)
  • Whole Milk (hot, to top)

Steps

  1. Warm a mug with boiling water, then discard water.
  2. Add rum, cognac and batter into the cup and top with hot milk.
  3. Garnish with nutmeg, clove and allspice.
