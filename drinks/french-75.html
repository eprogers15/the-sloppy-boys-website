French 75

Ingredients

  • Champagne (2oz/60ml)
  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Lemon Juice (1oz/30ml)
  • Simple Syrup (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients, except champagne, into a shaker.
  2. Shake well and strain into a champagne flute.
  3. Top up with champagne.
  4. Garniish with lemon twist.
