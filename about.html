Sloppy Drinks is a website started by Emmett Rogers as a project to learn web development. The inspiration for the project was The Sloppy Boys Podcast, a cocktail podcast hosted by The Sloppy Boys band members Mike Hanford, Tim Kalpakis, and Jefferson Dutton. The goal of this site is to provide a quick and easy reference for all of the drinks covered on the podcast. Over time, the hope is to add features like sort, filter, and search.

Please feel free to send questions, comments, or suggestions to emmett@sloppydrinks.com

Emmett lives with his "lovely jackass wife" Amy in Philadelphia, PA.
