Trinidad Sour

Ingredients

  • Angostura Bitters (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Orgeat Syrup (1oz/30ml)
  • Lemon Juice (.76oz/22.5ml)
  • Rye Whiskey (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour all ingredients into cocktail shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake well and strain into chilled cocktail glass.
