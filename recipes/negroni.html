Negroni

Ingredients

  • Gin (1oz/30ml)
  • Bitter Campari (1oz/30ml)
  • Sweet Red Vermouth (1oz/30ml)

Steps

  1. Pour ingredients into chilled old fashioned glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Garnish with half orange slice.
