Tequila Sunrise

Ingredients

  • Tequila (1.5oz/45ml)
  • Orange Juice (3oz/90ml)
  • Grenadine Syrup (.5oz/15ml)

Steps

  1. Pour tequila and orange juice directly into highball glass filled with ice cubes.
  2. Add grenadine and stir gently to create gradient effect.
  3. Garnish with orange slice or zest.
