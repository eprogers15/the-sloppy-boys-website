Mimosa

Ingredients

  • Orange Juice (2.5oz/75ml)
  • Prosecco (2.5oz/75ml)

Steps

  1. Pour orange juice into flute glass and gently add prosecco.
  2. Stir gently.
  3. Garnish with orange twist.
